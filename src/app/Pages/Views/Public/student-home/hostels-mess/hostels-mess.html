Hostels & Mess Facilities

Hostel Facilities

CIPC provides comfortable and secure hostel accommodation for students. Our hostels are well-maintained with all essential amenities.

  • Separate hostels for boys and girls
  • 24/7 security and surveillance
  • Clean and hygienic rooms
  • Common areas and study rooms

Mess Facilities

Our mess provides nutritious and hygienic meals for all hostel residents. The menu is designed to provide balanced and healthy food options.

  • Fresh and hygienic food
  • Vegetarian and non-vegetarian options
  • Three meals per day
  • Special dietary requirements

Hostel Admission

For hostel admission and mess facility details, please contact the hostel warden or visit the administrative office. Hostel seats are limited and allocated on a first-come-first-serve basis.
