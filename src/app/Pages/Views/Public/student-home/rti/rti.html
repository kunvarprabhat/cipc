Right to Information

About RTI

The Right to Information Act, 2005 empowers citizens to seek information from public authorities. CIPC is committed to transparency and provides information as per RTI guidelines.

  • Transparent information access
  • Timely response to queries
  • Public information disclosure

RTI Application

To file an RTI application, please submit your request in writing along with the prescribed fee. Applications can be submitted in person or by post.

Application Fee: As per RTI Act 2005

Response Time: 30 days from receipt

Public Information Officer (PIO)

For RTI queries and applications, please contact the designated Public Information Officer at CIPC. All RTI applications are processed as per the guidelines of the RTI Act 2005.
