# CIPC Logo Usage Guide

## Logo Files Location
All logo files should be placed in: `src/Assets/logos/` and `public/Assets/logos/`

## Required Logo Files (8-10 variations):

### 1. logo-main.png
- **Size**: 800x800px
- **Use**: Homepage hero, About page, Main displays
- **Background**: Transparent or white

### 2. logo-header.png  
- **Size**: 200x60px (horizontal)
- **Use**: Navigation header, top bar
- **Background**: Transparent

### 3. logo-footer.png
- **Size**: 150x150px
- **Use**: Footer section
- **Background**: Transparent

### 4. logo-icon.png
- **Size**: 64x64px
- **Use**: Mobile header, small spaces, app icons
- **Background**: Transparent

### 5. logo-square.png
- **Size**: 200x200px
- **Use**: Dashboard header, cards, profile sections
- **Background**: Transparent

### 6. logo-white.png
- **Size**: 200x200px
- **Use**: Dark backgrounds, overlays
- **Background**: Transparent (white logo on transparent)

### 7. logo-transparent.png
- **Size**: 200x200px
- **Use**: Image overlays, watermarks
- **Background**: Fully transparent

### 8. logo-favicon.png
- **Size**: 32x32px
- **Use**: Browser favicon, tab icon
- **Background**: Transparent

### 9. logo-banner.png
- **Size**: 800x200px (wide format)
- **Use**: Banner sections, wide displays
- **Background**: Transparent

### 10. logo-certificate.png
- **Size**: 400x400px
- **Use**: Certificates, official documents
- **Background**: Transparent

## Current Logo Implementation:

✅ **Header (Public)**: Uses `logo-header.png` (desktop) and `logo-icon.png` (mobile)
✅ **Footer**: Uses `logo-footer.png`
✅ **Dashboard Header**: Uses `logo-square.png`
✅ **Favicon**: Uses `logo-favicon.png`

## Logo Design Specifications:

Based on the CIPC logo description:
- **Main Circle**: Yellow background (#FFD700 or similar)
- **Text**: "CIPC PARAMEDICAL COUNCIL" in dark blue
- **Inner Elements**: 
  - Caduceus symbol (golden)
  - Open book (white/brown)
  - Laurel wreaths (green)
  - Stars (green)
- **Banner**: Yellow with Devanagari text "सर्व सम्भाव्यते त्वा यि"
